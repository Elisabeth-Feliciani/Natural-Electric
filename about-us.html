About Us


Natural Electric is a leading sole proprietorship dedicated to providing exceptional electrical services to commercial, residential, and industrial clients. With our unwavering commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the electrical contracting industry. Since our inception, we have been illuminating the world with our expertise, professionalism, and a passion for delivering innovative electrical solutions.

Our Story:
Natural Electric is the brainchild of Adam Feliciani, an experienced and highly skilled electrician with a vision to create a company that would redefine excellence in electrical services. With a strong belief in the power of superior craftsmanship, integrity, and exceptional customer service, our founder embarked on a journey to establish a company that would surpass industry standards.
Over the years, Natural Electric has grown from a one-person operation to a thriving enterprise, serving a diverse range of clients across Caledon, York Region and the Greater Toronto Area. Despite our expansion, we have stayed true to our roots, maintaining the same dedication, attention to detail, and personal touch that set us apart from the competition.

Our Approach:
At Natural Electric, we believe that every electrical project deserves individualized attention. We take the time to understand our clients' needs, offering tailored solutions that address their unique requirements. From the initial consultation to project completion, our team of skilled electricians works closely with clients, ensuring clear communication, timely delivery, and complete satisfaction.

Our Services:
We offer a comprehensive range of electrical services, spanning installation, repair, maintenance, and upgrades. Whether it's a small residential wiring job, a complex commercial project, or an industrial facility overhaul, Natural Electric has the expertise, resources, and experience to handle it all. Industrial Electrical Services:
